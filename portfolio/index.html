Così lontano, così vicino / editorial project / 2013

Re-contestualization of a masterpiece by Mimmo Castellano, photographer and graphic designer, titled "Paese Lucano". We had the luck to meet him personally, and to have his original pictures available to re-edit his photo book. Our aim was to combine his pictures with quotes and old prints regarding Southern Italy, so that, all together, they made up a sort of encyclopedian work. Group work with Veronica Maccari, Martina Micheli and Valentina Rocchetti.

“This operation would have given new life to those object lying in farmers' houses, and to those incredible landscapes which I discovered as treasures, and that I greedily stored in my pictures”

– Mimmo Castellano about Paese Lucano
